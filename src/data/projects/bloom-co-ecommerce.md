## The Challenge

Bloom & Co had a loyal customer base built on Instagram and farmer's markets, but their online store — a basic Shopify theme — wasn't converting new visitors. The brand felt premium in person but generic online. They also had no mechanism for recurring revenue, leaving significant money on the table.

## Our Approach

### Brand-Aligned Design System

We built a custom **Next.js storefront** connected to Shopify's Storefront API — giving us full design freedom without sacrificing Shopify's inventory and payment infrastructure. Every component was built from scratch to reflect Bloom & Co's aesthetic: warm earth tones, soft typography, and botanical illustration accents.

### The Skin Quiz

The centerpiece of the experience was a **personalized skin quiz** — a 5-question flow that recommended a tailored product bundle. This served two purposes:

1. **Reduced decision paralysis** for new customers unfamiliar with the range
2. **Increased AOV** by presenting curated bundles rather than single products

Quiz results were stored and used to personalize return visits and email flows through Klaviyo.

### Subscribe & Save

We architected a subscription layer on top of Shopify using **Recharge**, allowing customers to subscribe to their favorite products at a 15% discount. A custom subscription dashboard let them pause, swap products, or skip deliveries — dramatically reducing churn.

## The Result

The new store launched to Bloom & Co's email list and immediately outperformed expectations. Conversion rate climbed to **4.7%** — more than double the industry average. The subscribe & save program generated **$22k in new MRR** within its first quarter.

The skin quiz became a viral sharing tool when customers started posting their personalized results on TikTok, driving organic acquisition.
