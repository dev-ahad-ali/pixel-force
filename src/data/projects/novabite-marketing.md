## The Challenge

NovaBite had 8 locations across three cities and was spending $15k/month on advertising with inconsistent results. They had no unified tracking, each location was running its own social media sporadically, and their loyalty program had only 3,000 members despite years of operation.

They needed a centralized, data-driven marketing engine.

## Our Approach

### Audit & Strategy

We started with a full marketing audit: ad account history, website analytics, customer data, and competitor positioning. The findings were clear — most of their ad spend was going to broad awareness campaigns with no clear funnel structure and no remarketing.

### Funnel Architecture

We rebuilt their paid media strategy around a **three-stage funnel**:

**Top of Funnel (Awareness)**
- Video ads showcasing food preparation and atmosphere, targeted to lookalike audiences of existing customers
- Geo-targeted campaigns within 5 miles of each restaurant location

**Middle of Funnel (Consideration)**
- Retargeting website visitors with menu highlight ads
- Google Search campaigns capturing high-intent queries like "best [cuisine] near me"

**Bottom of Funnel (Conversion)**
- Loyalty program sign-up campaigns with a first-order discount offer
- Abandoned cart SMS sequences for online order drop-offs

### Content & Creative

We produced a monthly **content calendar** spanning Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook. Short-form video of kitchen prep and customer reactions drove the highest organic reach. User-generated content was repurposed into paid creative, cutting production costs while improving authenticity.

### Loyalty Program Revamp

The loyalty program was re-launched with a new points structure and integrated with **Klaviyo** for automated email flows: welcome series, birthday rewards, lapsed customer win-backs, and post-visit review requests.

## The Result

Within 6 months, online orders increased **189%** year-over-year. ROAS across all paid channels reached **7.4x**, up from 2.1x previously. The loyalty program grew from 3,000 to over 40,000 members, creating a powerful owned-channel asset for future campaigns.

NovaBite expanded to two additional locations in the following quarter, citing increased demand as a key factor.
