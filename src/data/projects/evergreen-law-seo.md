## The Challenge

Evergreen Law Group is a boutique firm specializing in estate planning and family law. Despite having excellent client reviews and experienced attorneys, they were virtually invisible online. Their website had thin content, zero backlinks, and a technical foundation full of crawl errors.

Paid ads were too expensive in the legal vertical ($80–200 per click), so organic search was the only sustainable acquisition channel.

## Our Approach

### Technical SEO Foundation

Before creating a single piece of content, we needed to fix what was broken. A full technical audit revealed:

- **47 broken internal links**
- No XML sitemap submitted to Google
- Missing or duplicate meta descriptions on 60% of pages
- Slow page load times (LCP of 6.2 seconds)
- No schema markup for the business or attorney profiles

We resolved all technical issues in the first month, establishing a clean foundation.

### Keyword Research & Content Mapping

Using **Ahrefs** and **Semrush**, we identified a universe of 280 relevant keywords. We segmented them into:

- **High-intent transactional** (e.g., "estate planning lawyer [city]") — target with service pages
- **Informational** (e.g., "how to write a will in [state]") — target with blog content
- **Local modifiers** — target with Google Business Profile and location pages

We mapped every keyword to an existing or new page to avoid cannibalization.

### Content Production

We published **4 long-form articles per month**, each optimized with **Surfer SEO** for topical depth and keyword coverage. Articles were reviewed by the firm's attorneys for accuracy — a key trust signal for both readers and Google's E-E-A-T guidelines.

Cornerstone pieces included comprehensive guides like "The Complete Guide to Estate Planning in [State]" — which became the top-ranking piece and a major source of backlinks.

### Link Building

We executed a **digital PR and resource link-building campaign**:
- Contributed expert quotes to legal and financial publications
- Created linkable data assets (survey on estate planning awareness)
- Built local citations across 80+ directories
- Pursued broken link building in the legal and personal finance niche

## The Result

Over 12 months, Evergreen Law achieved #1 rankings for 14 of their target keywords. Organic traffic grew **340%** year-over-year, and the site now consistently generates **45+ qualified leads per month** from organic search alone — at zero ongoing ad spend.

The firm's managing partner reported that SEO had become their highest-ROI marketing investment by month 9.
