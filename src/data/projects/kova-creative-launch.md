## The Challenge

Kova Creative's founder — a seasoned brand strategist leaving a large agency — was launching her own consultancy. She had 30 days before she planned to announce on LinkedIn and needed a brand and website that could stand against established agencies. First impressions were everything, and there was no room for "we're working on it."

## Our Approach

### Sprint Structure

Given the aggressive timeline, we ran the project as a **4-week brand sprint**:

- **Week 1:** Strategy, positioning, naming rationale, moodboards
- **Week 2:** Logo design — two directions, one round of revisions
- **Week 3:** Full identity rollout (color, type, brand elements, stationery), website wireframes
- **Week 4:** Website design and development, content integration, launch

### Brand Strategy

The founder's unique positioning was her ability to bring large-agency thinking to small and mid-sized businesses — strategic rigor without enterprise overhead. We distilled this into a brand platform built around the idea of *"decisive clarity"* — a brand for clients who are tired of vague strategy decks and want decisions.

The name **Kova** (derived from the Finnish word for "solid" or "firm") reflected this positioning — short, memorable, and meaning-rich.

### Logo & Identity

The logo was a bespoke **geometric wordmark** — the 'K' constructed from precise angular geometry that suggested both structure and forward movement. The identity system extended this geometry into a flexible pattern system used across presentations, social media, and document headers.

Color palette:
- Obsidian `#1A1A2E` — authority and depth
- Chalk `#F0EFE9` — warmth and approachability
- Acid Yellow `#E8FF3B` — energy, differentiation, modernity

The acid yellow became a signature — used sparingly as a highlight color that made every application immediately recognizable.

### Website

The Webflow site was built with **GSAP scroll animations** — subtle but sophisticated. Key pages included:

- **Home** — full-screen hero with animated tagline, services overview, selected work preview
- **Work** — project case study grid with category filtering
- **About** — founder story, process, values
- **Contact** — direct, friction-free

Copy was written in-house in close collaboration with the founder, capturing her voice: direct, thoughtful, occasionally dry.

## The Result

Kova launched to the founder's LinkedIn network and the response was immediate. The brand was perceived as established and premium despite being day-one. Within **30 days, three new clients had signed** — two of which mentioned the website as a key trust factor in their decision. The launch was featured in two design-focused publications as a standout brand debut of the month.

The project demonstrated that a compressed timeline, when structured well, can produce exceptional results.
